About Me

Data isn’t just my job; it’s how I think, explore, and create.

I'm a data nerd with a strong foundation in analytics and an unshakable passion for solving real-world problems with data. I earned my Master’s from the Institute for Advanced Analytics at NC State, where I was trained to approach any business challenge with the mindset of a data-driven problem solver. Whether it's building predictive models, uncovering patterns in messy datasets, or turning complex insights into clear, actionable strategies — I’m all in.

Outside of the world of data, I’m a big believer in living fully and intentionally. I’m passionate about family, weekends spent around the Traeger perfecting my burnt ends recipe, and diving deep into real estate investing. When I’m not crunching numbers, you’ll probably find me watching a Dodgers game or yelling at the TV during an Alabama football game (Roll Tide!). I bring the same energy and curiosity to my personal passions as I do to every data project I take on.

Check Out Some Skills In My Tool Box!

From Python to storytelling—here are the skills that turn messy data into smart decisions.

Data Wrangling

This is the foundation of a solid project. Most of the real work happens before the modeling—cleaning, restructuring, and validating data. I develop a unique process for each project to handle everything from missing values to inconsistent formats and schema mismatches. Strong data wrangling ensures my analyses are built on a reliable foundation.

Data Storytelling

Raw numbers don’t move people—stories do. I focus on translating complex analyses into clear, engaging visuals and narratives that drive action. Whether it's a stakeholder presentation or dashboard design, I make sure the message behind the data is easy to understand, impactful, and—most importantly—tailored to the audience.

Machine Learning & AI

I apply machine learning models to identify patterns, make predictions, and automate decision-making. From logistic regression to tree-based models and beyond, I’ve trained, tuned, and deployed models in real-world projects. I also explore and develop AI tools to augment workflows and prototype solutions quickly.

Python

My go-to language for data science tasks, from data wrangling and analysis to building machine learning pipelines. I regularly use libraries like Pandas, NumPy, and Scikit-learn to explore data and create efficient, scalable solutions. Its versatility makes it a cornerstone of my analytics toolbox.

R-Studio

I use this for powerful statistical analysis and data visualization. I use it when working with exploratory data analysis, forecasting, and building clean, publication-ready visuals using ggplot2 and tidyverse packages. It's a great complement to Python when deep statistical rigor is needed.

SQL

This is essential for querying structured data and working directly with databases. I use it to filter, join, and aggregate large datasets to uncover trends and drive analysis. Whether it’s cleaning raw transactional data or feeding dashboards, SQL is at the center of my data extraction workflow.
